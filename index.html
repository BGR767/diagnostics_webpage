About Us

We are a leading Indian company specializing in the distribution and supply of point-of-care diagnostic devices. Our mission is to make high-quality diagnostics accessible, affordable, and available at the last mile.

We work closely with multiple State Governments and public health institutions to deploy, operate, and maintain diagnostic devices in both urban and remote regions across the country.

By bridging the gap between advanced diagnostics and underserved communities, we are helping build a more equitable and resilient healthcare system in India.
